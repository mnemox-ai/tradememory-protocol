## 3. SaaS vs Open Source + Premium

| Dimension | Pure SaaS | Open Source + Premium |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Revenue start** | Day 1 (paywall) | Delayed (build trust first) |

### 4.2 Willingness to Pay

| Factor | Crypto Traders | Forex Traders |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| **Tool spending** | $30-150/mo (bots, signals) | $0-60/mo (mostly free via broker) |
| **Payment friction** | Low (used to crypto payments) | Medium (traditional payment methods) |
| **Free alternatives** | Many (Pionex, DEX bots) | Many (MT4/MT5 free EAs) |
| **Subscription tolerance** | Higher (used to SaaS model) | Lower (expect broker to provide tools) |
| **Community engagement** | Very high (Discord, Telegram) | Medium (forums, MT5 community) |
| **Decision speed** | Fast (FOMO-driven) | Slow (research-driven) |
| **LTV potential** | Lower (high churn, market-dependent) | Higher (steady, professional use) |
| **Market size** | Smaller but growing fast | Massive but mature |

### 4.3 Key Insight

**Crypto traders pay more readily but churn faster.** 平均 LTV 可能相近。

**Forex traders 難轉換但一旦用了就不走。** MT5/MT4 生態有 lock-in effect。

**For TradeMemory:**
- Short-term revenue: Crypto traders (easier to reach via Discord/Telegram/GitHub)
- Long-term value: Forex/institutional (higher LTV, willing to pay for infrastructure)
- Sweet spot: **Developer-focused pricing** — 不是賣給 end-user trader，是賣給 **building trading agents 的開發者**

## 6. TradeMemory Pricing Recommendations

### 6.1 Proposed Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Community** | Free (MIT) | Individual devs, students | 15 MCP tools, SQLite, local only, full OWM |
| **Pro** | $29-49/mo | Indie traders, small teams | Cloud hosted, multi-agent sync, advanced reflection, priority support |
| **Team** | $99-199/mo | Trading desks, prop firms | Team dashboards, shared memory, API access, SLA |
| **Enterprise** | Custom ($500+/mo) | Institutions, hedge funds | On-prem deployment, custom integrations, dedicated support |

### 6.2 Rationale

1. **Free tier 要強** — 開源是 moat，不是 cost center。所有 MCP tools 免費用。靠 GitHub stars 和 community 長大。
2. **Pro tier 對標 $29-49** — 低於 3Commas ($50) 和 Cryptohopper ($69)，但 TradeMemory 是 infrastructure 不是 end-user bot，定價要反映這個差異。
3. **Team/Enterprise 是真正的 revenue** — 機構願意付 $500+/mo 的 infrastructure fee（cf. QuantConnect, TrendSpider $197/mo）。
4. **不做 performance fee** — TradeMemory 不執行交易，收 performance fee 不合理。

## 7. Key Takeaways

1. **不要跟大詞打** — "AI trading bot" 和 "crypto trading bot" 被 affiliate site 壟斷，SEO 成本極高
2. **搶 "MCP trading" category** — 2026 年爆發，LSEG 都進場了，但內容還很少。現在搶 = first mover advantage
3. **"trading memory" 是獨佔詞** — Google 搜 "trading memory" 第一頁就是 TradeMemory。持續強化
4. **定價不要模仿 crypto bot** — TradeMemory 是 developer infrastructure，不是 retail bot。對標 QuantConnect 不是 3Commas
5. **Crypto 先行，Forex 後攻** — Crypto 社群更願意嘗試新工具，Forex 用戶忠誠但轉換慢
6. **免費+開源是正確策略** — 在競爭激烈的市場裡，MIT license + 強 free tier 是最好的 distribution strategy
7. **MCP 生態會是 TradeMemory 最大的 distribution channel** — 隨著更多 agent 框架支援 MCP，TradeMemory 自然被發現
